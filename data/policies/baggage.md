# CloudSeven Airlines — Baggage Policy

## Cabin Baggage
All passengers may carry one piece of cabin baggage plus one personal item (laptop bag, handbag, or small backpack).

- **Maximum dimensions:** 55 × 35 × 25 cm
- **Maximum weight:** 7 kg (Economy / Premium Economy), 10 kg (Business / First)

## Checked Baggage Allowance

| Cabin Class | Domestic | International (short-haul) | International (long-haul) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Economy | 1 × 15 kg | 1 × 23 kg | 1 × 30 kg |
| Premium Economy | 1 × 20 kg | 1 × 30 kg | 2 × 23 kg |
| Business | 2 × 23 kg | 2 × 32 kg | 2 × 32 kg |
| First | 3 × 32 kg | 3 × 32 kg | 3 × 32 kg |

## Excess Baggage
Charges apply per kilogram beyond the included allowance. Rates vary by route and can be viewed at the time of check-in.

## Restricted Items
Lithium batteries above 100 Wh, sharp objects, and flammable liquids are prohibited in checked baggage. Please consult our website for the full list.

## Lost or Delayed Baggage
Report immediately at the CloudSeven baggage desk before leaving the arrivals area, or call our 24/7 helpline. You will be issued a Property Irregularity Report (PIR).
