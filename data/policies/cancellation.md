# CloudSeven Airlines — Cancellation & Refund Policy

## 24-Hour Free Cancellation
Bookings made directly through CloudSeven (website, app, or call centre) can be cancelled within 24 hours of booking for a full refund, provided the departure is at least 7 days away.

## Standard Cancellation
After the 24-hour window, cancellation fees apply based on cabin class and how far in advance you cancel.

| Time Before Departure | Economy | Premium Economy | Business | First |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 7+ days | ₹3,000 | ₹2,000 | ₹1,000 | Free |
| 2-7 days | ₹5,000 | ₹4,000 | ₹2,500 | ₹1,500 |
| Under 48 hours | 50% of fare | 40% of fare | 25% of fare | 15% of fare |
| No-show | Non-refundable | Non-refundable | Non-refundable | Non-refundable |

## Refund Timeline
Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 7-10 business days for cards, and 5-7 business days for UPI/net banking.

## Schedule Changes by CloudSeven
If CloudSeven cancels your flight or changes the schedule by more than 4 hours, you are entitled to:
- A full refund with no fees, OR
- Rebooking on the next available CloudSeven flight at no additional cost.
