# CloudSeven Airlines — Travelling with Pets

## In-Cabin Pets
Small dogs and cats may travel in the cabin if the combined weight of the pet and carrier does not exceed 7 kg.

- Carrier must fit under the seat in front of you (max 40 × 30 × 20 cm).
- Pet must remain inside the carrier for the entire flight.
- Limit of 2 in-cabin pets per flight; advance booking required.

## Checked Baggage (Hold)
Pets weighing 7-32 kg (including carrier) may travel as checked baggage in the temperature-controlled hold.

## Cargo
Pets above 32 kg or travelling unaccompanied must book via CloudSeven Cargo.

## Service Animals
Trained service animals fly free of charge in the cabin with appropriate documentation. Please notify us at least 48 hours before departure.

## Prohibited Breeds
Snub-nosed breeds (Pugs, Bulldogs, Persian cats, etc.) are restricted from hold travel on flights longer than 4 hours due to respiratory risk.

## Documentation
All pets must have valid vaccination certificates and, for international travel, an import permit from the destination country.
