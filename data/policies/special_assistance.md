# CloudSeven Airlines — Special Assistance

CloudSeven is committed to making air travel accessible to all passengers. Please request assistance at least 48 hours before departure.

## Wheelchair Assistance
We provide wheelchair service from check-in to the aircraft door and onward to your arrival point. Three categories are available:
- **WCHR** — passenger can walk short distances and climb stairs
- **WCHS** — passenger cannot climb stairs but can walk to seat
- **WCHC** — passenger requires assistance to seat

## Visual or Hearing Impairment
Trained staff provide guided escort, safety briefings in accessible formats, and priority boarding.

## Medical Devices
Portable oxygen concentrators (POCs), CPAP machines, and battery-powered medical equipment are permitted. Please notify us in advance and carry a doctor's certificate where applicable.

## Unaccompanied Minors
Children aged 5-12 travelling alone must be booked on the Unaccompanied Minor (UMNR) service. Fee: ₹2,500 per sector domestic, ₹5,000 international.

## Expectant Mothers
Travel is permitted up to 36 weeks of pregnancy with a medical certificate dated within 7 days of departure for single pregnancies, or 32 weeks for multiple pregnancies.
